The Experience

Join us for our signature morning route along the scenic Donauinsel. Designed for intermediate runners, this 10km session focuses on consistent pacing and dynamic urban views as the city wakes up.

We'll maintain a comfortable 5:30 min/km pace. The route is entirely flat, utilizing the premium paved paths of the island, ensuring a smooth and uninterrupted flow. Post-run, the group will gather for espresso at a nearby curated local café.
